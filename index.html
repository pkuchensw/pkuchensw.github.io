Research Experience

LLM Reinforcement Learning System Design

Advisor: Prof. Bin Cui and Prof. Xupeng Miao

May 2025 – Present

Reinforcement learning has become a cornerstone for improving the reasoning abilities of large language models, but training efficiency is often limited by the straggler effect caused by long-tail generations.

  • Proposed DARTS, a distribution-aware active rollout trajectory shaping paradigm.
  • Designed Dual End Sampling to encourage concise output distributions.
  • Integrated adaptive redundant allocation with token-level streaming to accelerate RL training while maintaining reasoning capacity.

Efficient LLM Reinforcement Learning Algorithm

Advisor: Prof. Bin Cui and Prof. Xupeng Miao

Dec 2025 – Mar 2026
  • Studied length explosion in large reasoning models during RL training.
  • Proposed QLPO, a resampling-based variant of GRPO for implicit length control.
  • Improved the accuracy-length trade-off across models from 1.5B to 32B, reducing response length by 30%–70% while maintaining or improving reasoning performance.
